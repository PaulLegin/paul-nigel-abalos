About

Technology with academic purpose and real-world impact.

I am an Assistant Professor in Information Technology at Pangasinan State University – San Carlos Campus, where I teach, conduct research, and develop technology solutions that bridge academia and industry. I welcome opportunities for research collaboration, academic partnerships, technology projects, and professional networking.
